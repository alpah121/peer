Peer To Peer Dapp Loans Design Page

Brainstorming

  • Secured with Dapp smart contract and front end code
  • keys are given to us and distributed using blockedChain
  • loan amount calculated with the mean of verified customers
  • four step process: quote, security audit, phone call, get paid
  • you can take a loan against what you invested
  • we take 1% and the investor charges 9%
  • term lengths are in multiples of 3

Front end

  • explain four step process
  • questionare about the business
  • have an investors page explaining how we secure the loans and what happens when someone defaults

Smart Contract

  • collects payments on the loan
  • transfers ownership of business when three payments are missed in a row
  • stores the payments into investor accounts
  • gives loan to investor depending on how far along the business loan is
  • transfers owners fee to owner

Back End

  • scan previous blocks for transactions to the business
  • use blockedChain to store keys
  • pings smart contracts every month
  • creates smart contract using part of application fee
